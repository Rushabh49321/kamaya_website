Our Machine



Weave Preparatory Unit

• An automated PLC-controlled beam sizing machine with a working width of 280 cm that can size 8-10 tons of beams in a day. It has two-size boxes, 12 dry cylinders, and 16 beam creels.

• There are two direct warping systems: a turn table creel and an H-creel that can hold 630 ends. Both include wrap stop-motion systems and a drum-driven warping headstock that can reach a speed of 600 m/min.

• A PLC-controlled section for warping that produces colored and patterned fabric beaming with a turn table creel capacity of 480 ends, stop motion, and a drum working speed of up to 700 m/min.

• A 1.5-tonne boiler unit, which runs on sustainable bio-coal, to help with the sizing process.

Weaving Unit

• 72 Rapier Looms with working widths of 160, 280, and 360 cm and fast operating speeds were designed to provide accuracy during the weaving of fabrics.

• Modern technology is incorporated into 12 auto shuttle change looms, which eliminates the need for time-consuming shuttle changes compared to classic shuttle looms and simplify the operation of several looms at once.

• To guarantee the precise level of humidity suitable for the type of fabric to be weaved a humification plant has been placed across the weaving area.

• A folding machine that achieves precise cloth folding that is efficient, time-saving, and quick.
