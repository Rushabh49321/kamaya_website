Our Background



Who we are ?

One of India's top producers of textiles, Kamya Textiles Mills is a renowned textile manufacturer and exporter located in Rajkot, Gujarat. founded in 2016. It's a business with a lot of promise in the textile manufacturing industry.

To fulfill changing orders on schedule and with the ideal balance of quality and efficiency, we have implemented a flexible manufacturing chain.

In 2016, Kamya Textile Mills was created. To be technologically advanced on a worldwide scale, we have increased our capacity, trained our workers, and implemented integrated technology.

In 2023, we have succeeded in completing seven years. We will keep innovating and working to raise the bar for quality in the Indian textile sector.

What we do ?

Making the best high-quality products possible is more than just a business objective for us; it's a dedication to excellence woven throughout the entire structure of our organization. Our commitment to clarity establishes a benchmark for openness and transparency, guaranteeing that our clients can rely on the caliber and workmanship of each product we make. Every product we produce sets the standard for others in the industry to follow and is a testament to our everlasting commitment to perfection.

Beyond producing outstanding products, our goal is to serve as a source of motivation for ambitious companies and entrepreneurs. We think that our success story might inspire others by demonstrating that anyone can create a successful business with hard work and perseverance. Furthermore, we acknowledge the distinct obstacles faced by the textile sector and are committed to executing inventive resolutions that cater to our clients' daily requirements. Our goal is to reinvent the textile industry's standards of excellence by constant innovation and a customer-centric strategy, which will not only help us succeed but also improve the industry overall.
