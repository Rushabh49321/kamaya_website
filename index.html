Jute

Jute fabrics, derived from the natural fibers of the jute plant, boast a unique blend of strength and eco-friendliness. Known for their coarse texture, jute fabrics are often utilized in the creation of sturdy bags

Cotton Fabric

Cotton fabrics are soft, breathable textiles made from natural cotton fibers, renowned for their comfort and versatility. With excellent moisture absorption properties, cotton fabrics keep the body cool in warm weather

Grey Fabric

Grey fabrics, often woven from a blend of different fibers, offer a neutral and timeless aesthetic. These textiles come in various shades of grey, providing a versatile choice for fashion and interior design

Juco Fabric

Cotton jute fabrics combine the natural softness of cotton with the rustic texture of jute fibers. This blend creates textiles that are both comfortable and visually appealing. Cotton adds a smooth touch to the coarser jute.

Organic Fabric

Organic fabrics are derived from natural fibers cultivated without the use of synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms. Popular materials for organic textiles include cotton, hemp, and linen.
